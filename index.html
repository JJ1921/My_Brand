About Us



Welcome to preFixed, where the passion for cycling meets quality craftsmanship. We are your premier destination for high-quality fixed gear bikes and an active fixed gear community in the heart of the Philippines.


At preFixed, we live and breathe cycling. Our journey began with a deep love for the simplicity, elegance, and sheer joy of fixed gear riding. We are committed to providing you with the best riding experience possible, whether you're a seasoned cyclist or just starting your two-wheeled adventure.


Our shop is a treasure trove of top-notch fixed gear bikes, accessories, and components. We curate our selection to cater to the diverse needs of cyclists. Whether you're an urban commuter navigating the bustling streets of Manila or a competitive racer seeking that extra edge, we have the perfect bike for you. Our bikes are meticulously crafted to deliver exceptional performance and style.



But we're more than just a bike shop; we're a community. Our vision is to foster a strong and vibrant fixed gear community. We believe in the power of cycling to bring people together, and our community is a place to connect with fellow riders, share unforgettable experiences, and stay updated on the latest trends and events in the world of fixed gear biking.


Join us in celebrating the cycling lifestyle. We organize group rides, workshops, and events that encourage camaraderie and growth within the fixed gear community. Whether it's a leisurely ride along scenic routes or a friendly competition to test your skills, there's always something exciting happening at preFixed.


At preFixed, we're not just selling bikes; we're sharing a passion. We're dedicated to sustainability and a healthier future. We believe that every pedal stroke contributes to a greener world. Let's embark on this incredible journey together, pedal by pedal, as we strive for a healthier, more sustainable, and exciting future on two wheels.
